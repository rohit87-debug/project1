Software development

Software development is the process of creating, designing, testing, and maintaining computer programs and applications to meet user needs or business goals. This comprehensive process goes beyond just coding and includes all steps from initial concept and requirements analysis to deployment and ongoing maintenance, often following a structured software development lifecycle (SDLC). It requires a combination of computer science principles, creativity, and problem-solving skills.


Ai/ML

AIML can stand for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, a field of study and engineering focused on creating intelligent systems that learn from data, or Artificial Intelligence Markup Language, an XML-based language used for creating chatbots and other natural language software agents. The specific meaning depends on the context, whether you are referring to an academic program, a career path, or the technology behind a chatbot.


Data analyses

Data analysis is the process of collecting, cleaning, transforming, and modeling data to discover useful information, find patterns, and support decision-making. It involves using statistical and logical techniques to interpret data and turn it into actionable insights for businesses or scientific research. Key steps include identifying the data, cleaning it, analyzing it, and interpreting the results.
